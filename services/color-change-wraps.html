Premium Vehicle Wrapping

Color change wraps offer a dramatic transformation without the permanence of a respray. At Tint My Ride, we use premium-grade vinyl wraps from leading manufacturers, available in a vast spectrum of colors and finishes including matte, gloss, satin, metallic, chrome, and textured finishes.

Whether you want to give your daily driver a bold new look, protect a classic car's original paint, or create a unique business vehicle, our certified installers deliver flawless results at our Shuwaikh Industrial studio.

  • Hundreds of colors, finishes, and textures to choose from
  • Protects original paint while transforming your car's look
  • Fully reversible — return to factory paint at any time
  • Premium-grade vinyl with UV stability for Kuwait's climate
  • Faster turnaround than a full paint job
WHY WRAP

Benefits of Color Change Wraps

  • Complete Transformation — Change your car's color completely without the cost and permanence of a repaint. Go from silver to matte black or any color imaginable.
  • Paint Protection — Wraps protect your original paint from UV rays, minor scratches, and chips. When removed, the factory paint underneath is preserved.
  • Cost-Effective — A high-quality wrap costs significantly less than a professional respray while delivering stunning results.
  • Reversible — Tired of the color? The wrap can be removed without damaging the original paint, giving you the freedom to change styles whenever you want.
  • Custom Finishes — Achieve finishes that are difficult or impossible with paint, including matte, satin, chrome, carbon fiber, and textured effects.
  • Business Branding — Perfect for company fleets and commercial vehicles. Create a mobile billboard with custom graphics and branding.
    • OUR PROCESS

    How We Wrap Your Vehicle

    Professional installation for a flawless, long-lasting finish.

    01

    Consultation & Design

    We discuss your vision, show you samples, and help select the perfect color and finish. For custom designs, we create digital mockups.

    02

    Vehicle Preparation

    Your car is thoroughly washed, decontaminated, and any surface imperfections are addressed to ensure perfect adhesion.

    03

    Panel Removal (if needed)

    For a professional finish, certain trim pieces, lights, and handles may be removed to allow the wrap to tuck behind edges.

    04

    Vinyl Application

    Our certified installers apply the vinyl using precise techniques, heating and stretching the material to conform perfectly to every curve.

    05

    Post-Installation

    Trim pieces are reinstalled, edges are sealed, and the entire vehicle is inspected under controlled lighting.

    06

    Delivery & Care

    We provide full aftercare instructions including washing guidelines and what to avoid. Your wrap is ready to turn heads.
