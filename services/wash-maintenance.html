Regular Care for Lasting Beauty

At Legend PPF & Concept Chemicals, we believe that regular maintenance is the key to preserving your vehicle's appearance and value. Our wash and maintenance packages are designed to keep your car looking its best between full details, protecting your investment and ensuring you drive with pride every day.

Unlike automatic car washes that can damage your paint with harsh brushes and recycled water, our hand wash process is gentle, thorough, and uses only premium pH-neutral products. We treat every vehicle with the same care and attention we give our own.

  • pH-Neutral Hand Wash — Gentle, thorough cleaning that preserves wax and sealant layers
  • Interior Wipe-Down — Dash, console, door panels, and surfaces cleaned and protected
  • Wheel & Tire Cleaning — Brake dust removal and tire dressing for a complete finish
  • Glass Cleaning — Streak-free interior and exterior glass for crystal-clear visibility
  • Quick Detailing Spray — Light protection boost between wax applications

WHY REGULAR MAINTENANCE

Benefits of Regular Washing

  • Protects Your Paint — Regular washing removes contaminants that can etch into your clear coat over time.
  • Preserves Resale Value — A well-maintained exterior and interior commands a higher resale price.
  • Extends PPF & Coating Life — Regular care helps your PPF, ceramic coating, or wax last longer and perform better.
  • Healthier Interior — Regular vacuuming and cleaning reduce allergens, bacteria, and odors inside your car.
  • Pride of Ownership — Driving a clean, well-maintained car feels great and reflects your personal standards.
  • Cost-Effective — Regular maintenance prevents the need for expensive paint correction or deep cleaning later.
