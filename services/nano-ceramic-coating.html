What Is Nano Ceramic Coating?

Nano Ceramic Coating is a liquid polymer applied to the exterior of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the factory paint, creating a semi-permanent protective layer that is highly durable, hydrophobic, and UV-resistant.

At Tint My Ride, we use industry-leading ceramic coatings from Gtechniq. Our certified installers ensure flawless application in our Shuwaikh Industrial studio, delivering a showroom-quality finish that lasts for years.

Ceramic coating is the perfect complement to PPF and is ideal for clients who want long-lasting paint protection with minimal maintenance.

  • Extreme hydrophobic properties — water beads and rolls right off
  • Deep, mirror-like gloss that enhances your car's appearance
  • Chemical resistance against bird droppings, tree sap, and acid rain
  • UV protection prevents paint fading and oxidation
  • Easier to clean — dirt and grime struggle to stick
WHY CERAMIC

Benefits of Ceramic Coating

  • Long-Lasting Protection — Unlike wax or sealants that last weeks, ceramic coatings protect for 2-5 years depending on the product.
  • Hydrophobic Effect — Water, mud, and contaminants slide off effortlessly. Your car stays cleaner for longer between washes.
  • Enhanced Gloss — Ceramic coatings add incredible depth and shine, making your paint look deeper and richer than ever before.
  • Scratch Resistance — While not as impact-resistant as PPF, ceramic coatings provide a hard sacrificial layer that resists minor swirl marks.
  • Chemical Stain Protection — Protects against etching from bird droppings, bug splatters, and industrial fallout common in Kuwait.
  • Reduced Maintenance — Less frequent washing needed. When you do wash, contaminants release easily, reducing the risk of swirl marks.

    How We Apply Ceramic Coating

    Our meticulous preparation and application process ensures a perfect, long-lasting bond.

    01

    Deep Wash & Decontamination

    We perform a thorough wash, clay bar treatment, and chemical decontamination to remove all bonded contaminants from the paint.

    02

    Paint Correction

    Any swirl marks, scratches, or imperfections are carefully corrected. The coating magnifies existing flaws, so a flawless base is essential.

    03

    Panel Preparation

    Each panel is wiped down with a panel prep solution to remove any oils, waxes, or residues that could interfere with bonding.

    04

    Coating Application

    The ceramic coating is applied in a controlled environment using cross-hatch patterns for even coverage across every panel.

    05

    Curing Time

    The coating requires a curing period of 24-48 hours in our dust-free studio. During this time, it bonds permanently to the paint.

    06

    Inspection & Delivery

    We inspect the finish under specialized lighting, check for high spots, and provide detailed aftercare instructions before delivery.
