Premium Motorcycle Care

Your motorcycle deserves the same level of care and protection as any high-end automobile. At Legend PPF & Concept Chemicals, we offer specialized motorcycle protection and customization services tailored to the unique needs of bikes.

Whether you ride a sports bike, cruiser, touring machine, or off-road motorcycle, our certified technicians have the expertise to protect, enhance, and customize your ride. From full PPF coverage to custom color wraps, we treat every bike with the passion it deserves.

  • Motorcycle PPF for tank, fairings, fenders, and high-impact areas

  • Custom color change wraps for complete transformation
  • Professional detailing — both cosmetic and protective
  • UV protection for exposed plastic and painted surfaces
SERVICES

Motorcycle Services We Offer

Motorcycle PPF

Protect your bike's paint and expensive bodywork from stone chips, road debris, and abrasion. We apply precision-cut PPF to tanks, fairings, fenders, headlights, and any vulnerable surface.

Motorcycle Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating is ideal for motorcycles — it creates a hard, hydrophobic surface that repels water, dirt, and grime. Your bike stays cleaner longer and looks showroom-ready after every wash.

Motorcycle Detailing

Full cosmetic and protective detailing tailored for motorcycles. Includes deep cleaning, paint correction, polishing, and protection for painted, chrome, and plastic surfaces.

Custom Wraps & Graphics

Transform your bike's appearance with custom vinyl wraps and graphics. Available in any color or finish — matte, gloss, satin, metallic, or custom printed designs. Make your bike truly unique.
