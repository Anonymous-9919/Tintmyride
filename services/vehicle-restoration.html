Expert Vehicle Restoration

Whether you own a classic car that needs revival or a modern vehicle showing signs of wear, Tint My Ride offers comprehensive restoration services that breathe new life into your vehicle.

Our team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques and products to restore every aspect of your vehicle — from paintwork to interior, mechanical components to exterior trim. We treat every project with the care and attention it deserves.

Located in Shuwaikh Industrial, we serve clients across Kuwait who demand the highest standards of restoration for their prized vehicles.

  • Paint Restoration & Correction — Multi-stage polishing to remove oxidation, swirl marks, and imperfections
  • Interior Restoration — Leather repair, upholstery cleaning, headliner restoration, and trim refinishing
  • Headlight Restoration — Restore cloudy, yellowed headlights to crystal-clear condition
  • Trim & Plastic Restoration — Revive faded, discolored exterior trim and plastic components
  • Full Protection Package — PPF, ceramic coating, or wax protection applied after restoration
PROCESS

Our Restoration Process

A methodical approach to restoring your vehicle to its best condition.

01

Assessment & Consultation

We thoroughly inspect your vehicle, document all areas needing attention, and provide a detailed restoration plan with timeline and costs.

02

Deep Clean & Decontamination

The vehicle undergoes a comprehensive wash, clay bar treatment, and chemical decontamination to reveal the true condition of the surfaces.

03

Paint Correction

Multi-stage machine polishing removes swirl marks, scratches, oxidation, and water spots. We restore depth, gloss, and clarity to the paint.

04

Interior Restoration

Leather cleaning and conditioning, fabric shampooing, plastic restoration, and odor removal. Every interior surface is renewed.

05

Detail & Finishing

Headlights are restored, trim is treated, glass is polished, and all exterior surfaces are brought to a pristine finish.

06

Protection & Delivery

A final layer of protection (wax, sealant, or ceramic coating) is applied. We deliver your vehicle with care instructions and maintenance tips.
