EXTERIOR DETAILING

Exterior Detailing Services

Our exterior detailing services restore your vehicle's paintwork to a showroom-quality finish. We use advanced techniques and professional-grade products to remove contaminants, correct imperfections, and enhance gloss.

  • Hand Wash & Decontamination — pH-neutral hand wash, iron fallout remover, and clay bar treatment for a smooth, clean surface
  • Paint Correction — Multi-stage machine polishing to remove swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation
  • Wheel & Tire Detailing — Deep cleaning of wheels, calipers, and tire dressing for a complete finish
  • Glass & Trim Restoration — Clean and polish all glass, restore faded trim, and treat exterior plastics
  • Final Protection — Wax, sealant, or ceramic booster applied as a finishing layer of protection
INTERIOR DETAILING

Interior Detailing Services

Your car's interior deserves the same attention as its exterior. Our interior detailing services go beyond a simple vacuum — we deep clean, sanitize, and restore every surface to like-new condition.

  • Deep Vacuum & Steam Cleaning — Removal of dust, dirt, and allergens from carpets, seats, and hard-to-reach areas
  • Leather Cleaning & Conditioning — Gentle cleaning followed by nourishing conditioner to prevent cracking and fading
  • Fabric & Upholstery Shampoo — Professional shampooing to remove stains, odors, and embedded dirt
  • Dashboard & Trim Care — Clean and protect all interior plastics, vinyl, and trim surfaces
  • Smoke Odor Removal — Specialized ozone treatment and enzymatic cleaners to eliminate smoke odors permanently
PAINT CORRECTION

Paint Correction Services

Paint correction is the process of removing surface imperfections from your vehicle's clear coat to restore depth, clarity, and gloss. Whether you're preparing for PPF or ceramic coating, or simply want to restore your car's showroom shine, our paint correction services deliver exceptional results.

  • Swirl Mark Removal — Eliminates the spider-web effect caused by improper washing techniques
  • Scratch Reduction — Light to moderate scratches in the clear coat are safely polished out
  • Oxidation Removal — Restores faded and oxidized paint, common in Kuwait's harsh sun
  • Water Spot Removal — Hard water spots and mineral deposits are safely eliminated
  • Gloss Enhancement — Multi-stage polishing brings out the true depth and brilliance of your paint
SMOKE ODOR REMOVAL

Smoke Odor Removal

Smoke odor can be one of the most stubborn smells to eliminate from a vehicle. At Tint My Ride, we use a multi-stage approach that combines deep cleaning, enzymatic treatments, and professional ozone generators to completely neutralize smoke odors at the molecular level.

  • Deep Interior Cleaning — All fabric surfaces are professionally shampooed to remove trapped smoke particles
  • Enzymatic Treatment — Specialized cleaners break down odor-causing compounds at the source
  • HVAC System Cleaning — The air conditioning system is treated to eliminate smoke buildup in vents
  • Ozone Treatment — Professional ozone generators oxidize and neutralize remaining odors
  • Long-Lasting Freshness — Final protection with odor-absorbing treatments for lasting results
